Recommended Reading

Qualitative Analysis Software

The best texts on using software for qualitative analysis:

  • Jackson, Kristi, and Pat Bazeley. (2019) Qualitative Data Analysis with NVivo. 3rd ed. Thousand Oaks, CA: SAGE Publications Ltd.
    • PDF for Chapter 1 - ‘Where to begin?’
    • Click by click instructions accompanying the book.
  • Bazeley, Pat. (2017) ‘Using Qualitative Data Analysis Software (QDAS) to Assist Data Analyses’. Pp. 1–18 in Handbook of Research Methods in Health Social Sciences, edited by P. Liamputtong. Singapore: Springer.
    • Open access copy.

Qualitative Analysis - how to resources

Also useful as a basis for understanding qualitative analysis, coding, and writing up more generally:

  • Bazeley, Pat. 2020. Qualitative Data Analysis: Practical Strategies. 2nd ed. London: SAGE Pubications.
    • Hard-copies available in most university libraries.
  • Saldaña, Johnny. (2013) The Coding Manual for Qualitative Researchers. 2nd ed. London: Sage Publications Ltd.
    • An Introduction to Codes and Coding PDF of Chapter 1.

Examples of applied use of NVivo

  • Byrne, David. (2022) ‘A Worked Example of Braun and Clarke’s Approach to Reflexive Thematic Analysis’. Quality & Quantity 56(3):1391–1412. doi: 10.1007/s11135-021-01182-y.
  • Hutchison, Andrew John, Lynne Halley Johnston, and Jeff David Breckon. (2010) ‘Using QSR‐NVivo to Facilitate the Development of a Grounded Theory Project: An Account of a Worked Example’. International Journal of Social Research Methodology 13(4):283–302. doi: 10.1080/13645570902996301.
